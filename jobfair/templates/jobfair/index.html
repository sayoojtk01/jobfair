Pinnacle'22

Pinnacle'22 will accrue the employers, experienced professionals and entry level talented individuals under one roof. This Job fair will also compile organizations from various sectors including all kinds of private organizations,highly competitive and professional job seekers thereby serving as the platform for an open and wide-ranging employment opportunities.

Pinnacle'22 is an event that gives students and employers a chance to meet one another, establish professional relationships and discuss potential job or internship opportunities.

This job fair will give equal opportunities to the jobseekers as well as to the employers. The concept is job seekers can meet multiple organization at one single place. It is a concept which is a big time saver for both job seekers and the organization. There is always a good chance of meeting many potential employers & highly skilled candidates at a single place.

Socializing with the employers is not as easy when you enter the market for a job search. Hence, accessibility of meeting multiple employers at a single place is benefit for both the organization as well as job seekers.
