Pinnacle '22

Pinnacle'22 will accrue the employers, experienced professionals and entry level talented individuals under one roof.This Job fair will also compile organizations from various sectors including all kinds of private organizations,highly competitive and professional job seekers thereby serving as the platform for an open and wide-ranging employment opportunities.

Employment is one of the basic needs in order to survive in the world. Employers and job seekers will be available in one place and the job seekers can meet the employers and get the chance to get their dream job. The main objective of Pinnacle 2022 is to provide employment to the unemployed youths of Kerala. Through this job fest, the job seekers can meet the employers and be able to grab the job of their choice.
